Blacklist 2019 TV-14, 7 Seasons

Raymond Reddington, who had disappeared twenty years earlier to become one of the FBI's Ten Most Wanted Fugitives, surrenders himself to the FBI. Taken to a black site, Reddington claims he wishes to help the FBI track down and apprehend the criminals and terrorists he spent the last twenty years associating with: individuals that are so devious that the US government is unaware of their very existence. He has two conditions: immunity from prosecution, and that he wants to work exclusively with Elizabeth Keen, a rookie profiler newly assigned to FBI.
